The Regenboog was great, we learned a lot of new poses.
But we went to the Ballenbak quickly, before the door closes.
From the Regenboog to the Ballenbak was a long sprint to rush.
We even got sick on the way, and had to barf in a bush.
But once we were in, we saw a lot of kakkers, clean shaven
They danced awfully bad and yelled: ‘’vo voor de leden’’.
We fought our way through to find some free space,
To dance and relax, and maybe find a familiar face.
We looked far and wide, and in a moment of peace,
There we found Mary… or was it Charlotte de Vries?
Wasn’t one of them off, protesting with her mates?
But the other was living far away in the States!
So which one was it? Can you tell me which friend?
Who we found and led this night to an end?

Charlotte
